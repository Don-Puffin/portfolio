About


Hello, I'm Aaron, a Kent based Web Developer!


I'm a creative web developer, dedicated to bringing you unique, tailor-made websites and apps.
I inject personality and style into your website, using my background in comedy to add fun and innovative new features along with eye-catching graphics.
Having lived in Japan for a number of years, I can help make your site more accessible to the Asian market.
I offer Japanese-English translation services.


History


Before becoming a web developer I performed with a comedy group and featured in projects around the UK. It was during one of these projects that I met and worked with Japanese performers who introduced me to their culture.
Our show was set to tour in Japan, but thanks to the pandemic this never happened. I moved to Japan in 2020 to experience life there for myself, and learnt Japanese whilst living and working in Osaka. I previously worked as a freelance script writer, focusing on history. However, I had always been interested in technology and software so I knew I wanted to pursue that. If the wildly inefficient fax machines and ancient computers of a Japanese office aren't enough to inspire you to take matters into your own hands then nothing will!
